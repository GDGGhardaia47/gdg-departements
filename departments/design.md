Welcome to the creative heart of the club! In the Design department, we don't just "make things pretty." We solve problems. We turn complex ideas into simple, beautiful visuals. Whether it’s an app screen that’s easy to use or an Instagram post that grabs attention, we are the ones who make the club look professional. No prior artistic talent is needed—just a willingness to learn the rules!

## 1. The Foundation (For All Department Members)
Before choosing a specialty, every member of the Design Department should masters these basics:
- **General Design Rules**: You will learn the "hidden laws" of design—Balance, Alignment, Contrast, and Hierarchy. You will learn why some designs look messy and others look professional.
- **Visual Inspiration**: How to build "Moodboards" and find high-quality inspiration so you never start from a blank page.
- **Figma Basics**: Everyone will learn how to set up and use Figma, the industry-standard tool.

## 2. The Specializations (Choose Your Path)
After the basics, you will focus on one of these two tracks:

### Track A: UI/UX Design
*   **Focus**: Designing the look and feel of mobile apps and websites before they are coded.
*   **What you will do**: Wireframing (blueprints), using Grids, and mastering Components.
*   **Key Projects**:
    - Live Demo: Building a Weather App prototype.
    - Competition: Creating a fully interactive Adhan App prototype.

### Track B: Graphic Design
*   **Focus**: Creating the marketing materials that the public sees on social media and at events.
*   **What you will do**: Social Media designs, Brand Identity, and Event Visuals.
*   **Key Projects**:
    - Designing Instagram posts that get engagement.
    - Creating Roll-ups and Banners for real events.
    - Logo creation basics.

## 3. What You Will Learn
- You won't just design "whatever." You will master Google’s Brand Guidelines and Material Design.
- Master the complete interface design workflow in Figma.
- Applying core design rules like balance, hierarchy, and typography to create clean visuals.
- How to produce high-quality work quickly when deadlines are tight.

## 4. Contribution to the Club
- You are the reason people stop scrolling to look at our posts and engage with our content.
- Developers cannot start coding until you provide the design files and assets.
- Your work ensures GDG Ghardaia maintains a world-class visual standard aligned with Google’s identity.
- From physical banners and badges to digital posts and stories, your work is the most visible part of every club event.
- Learning to give and receive constructive feedback without ego to improve your work.
- Building a strong portfolio of real-world club events to support your future career.

## 5. Career Impact (Future Jobs)
- **UI/UX Designer**: Designing apps for startups and companies (Very high demand).
- **Social Media Manager**: Handling visual content strategies.
- **Freelancer**: You can start taking paid design clients with these skills.
